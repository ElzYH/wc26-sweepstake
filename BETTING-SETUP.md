# 🏆 World Cup Sweepstake — How to Set Up Your Betting (Dead Simple)

Everyone has **5 free betting points** waiting. This takes about 2 minutes. Do it once.

**The only link you need:** **mandem.bbmsweepstake.co.uk/tracker**

---

## Step 1 — Claim your name (do this first)

1. Open **mandem.bbmsweepstake.co.uk/tracker** on your phone.
2. Tap the **Bets** tab.
3. **Pick your name** from the dropdown at the top.
4. In the passcode box, type a **passcode** you'll remember (**4–24 characters** — letters/numbers, your choice).
5. Tap **Create my passcode**.

✅ That's it — your name is now yours. Nobody else can bet with your points.

> ⚠️ **Whoever sets the passcode first owns that name.** So claim *your* name before someone grabs it. Pick something only you know.
> It's saved on your phone, so you only type it once on this device.

---

## Step 2 — Turn on phone alerts (goals, kick-offs, half-time, full-time)

**📱 iPhone — you MUST do this first or notifications will NOT work:**
1. In Safari, open the tracker, tap the **Share** button (the square with the up-arrow).
2. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Now open the sweepstake **from that new icon** on your home screen (not from Safari).

**Then (iPhone and Android):**
4. Tap the **⋯ menu** → **Get alerts** (or the 🔔 button).
5. Pick **your name** and tap to **turn on alerts**.
6. Tick the alerts you want: goals, kick-offs, half-time, full-time, knockouts, lead changes.

✅ Now your phone buzzes when your teams play and score.

---

## Step 3 — Place a bet

1. On the **Bets** tab, find a game that hasn't kicked off yet.
2. **Tap the odds** for who you think wins (or **Draw**).
3. Type how many points you want to **stake**.
4. Tap **Place bet**. Done.

- Want several games in one bet? Tap **🎰 Accumulator**, add up to 3 games — but **every** leg must win.
- **Bets lock at kick-off** and are **final** — no changing or cancelling once placed.
- A bet on a live game shows a **🔴 LIVE** badge with the running clock.

**Your free 5 points:** bet them on anything. **Win and the winnings are yours; lose and it costs you nothing.** After that you bet with the points your teams earn.

---

## 🎁 Free points (don't miss them)

A **🎁 banner** appears on the Bets tab when free points drop (the day before kick-off, then ~once a week). Tap **Claim** to grab them. One per person per drop.

---

## 😵 "Help, it's not working"

- **New phone / different device?** Open the tracker → Bets tab → pick your name → type the **same passcode** you chose before → tap **Save**. You're back in.
- **Forgot your passcode?** Ask the organiser (Erol) to reset it.
- **"Wrong passcode"?** It's case-sensitive-ish — type it exactly as you set it. A wrong one is never saved, so just try again.
- **No notifications on iPhone?** You skipped Step 2's "Add to Home Screen" — do that and open from the home-screen icon.

---

## (Optional) Bet from Discord instead

If you'd rather bet in Discord:
1. On the Bets tab, enter your passcode and tap **Connect Discord**.
2. It gives you a one-time code. In Discord, run: `/linkdiscord code:THATCODE`
3. After that, just use **`/bet`** — no passcode needed in Discord. Use **`/claim`** for free points.
