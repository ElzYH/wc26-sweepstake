What am I looking at?

  • Leaderboard — ranks players by the scoring mode chosen at setup.
  • Teams by player — each player's squad, with the points each team has earned.
  • Groups — the 12 World Cup group tables, with each team's owner.
  • Bracket — the knockout rounds, once the group stage ends.
  • Fixtures & times — the schedule with kickoff times in your timezone.
  • Odds & stats — each player's live chance of owning the eventual champion, plus fun numbers.

A team row in Teams by player: the number on the right is the points that team has earned you; the small "G" tag is its group (GK = Group K); a struck-through name means it has been knocked out.

Group tables: PLD = played, GD = goal difference, PTS = points. The top two of each group (highlighted) qualify automatically.

Leaderboard: "5/5 in" = how many of your teams are still alive. "0-0-0" = a team's wins-draws-losses.

Scoring modes — three ways to win:

  • Points — your teams earn points for goals, wins, clean sheets and for reaching each knockout round (plus, if betting is on, any betting winnings). It rewards doing well.
  • Survival — how far your teams go and how many are still alive. It rewards lasting — owning teams that keep advancing.
  • Both — literally Points + Survival added together for each player, then ranked once. You win it by having a squad that both racks up points and goes deep.

So why is someone's "Both" bigger than their "Points"? Because Both adds their Survival value on top. If a player owns the champion and other deep teams, their Survival can be a few hundred — so Both = Points + that. It's not double-counting and it's not a bug; the live example below shows the exact sum.

Fixtures: the bar under each match is the win chance — home / draw / away — from team strength, shifting toward the leader once a game is in-play.

Odds & stats: the win bars are your live chance of owning the champion (from bookmaker prices, updating as teams go out). The simulator there estimates your odds of winning on Points, Survival or Both.

📋 Summary: the quick digest at the top of Overview — leaders, teams left, top team and goals. The same digest can be posted to Discord from Settings.

⬇ Export: download the standings as a CSV — open it in Excel/Sheets or print it for the group.

🔔 Get alerts: tap it in the ⋯ menu, pick your name, and turn on alerts for your teams — goals, kick-offs, half-time, knockouts, lead changes and rivalries (when you overtake another player). Tick exactly which of those you want. If the daily summary is on, you'll also get your fixtures for the day each morning. On iPhone you must first add the site to your Home Screen (Share → Add to Home Screen) and open it from that icon; on a laptop just allow notifications. You can also join the group Discord, where the same alerts post for everyone.

Discord bot: in the group server, type / to pull live data — /leaderboard, /summary, /odds, /fixtures, /myteams, /help. It's read-only; nobody can change the draw from Discord.

The draw — Fair vs Weighted: Fair spreads the strongest teams evenly so everyone gets a balanced mix (within each strength band it's still random, so it's fair but not predictable). Weighted is luck-of-the-draw — a bigger team gives better odds but you might land several strong or several weak ones. On the Reveal page, Run draw on the server does the draw on the server and keeps going even if you close the tab — everyone can watch it live.

💷 Betting. Let players stake the points they've earned on games (fractional odds, capped, no cash-out). Off until you switch it on.

• Bet passcodes — each player gets a private code so only they can stake their own points. Generate them, then send each person their code. Players enter it once on the Bets tab (and once on Discord via /bet … pin:CODE).

🛑 Cancel bets. Cancel anyone's open bet — the stake is refunded and it counts as neither a win nor a loss. Bets on games that have already finished can't be cancelled.

Discord logins. Force everyone to log in with Discord again (e.g. a shared or lost device). Passcodes are unaffected.
